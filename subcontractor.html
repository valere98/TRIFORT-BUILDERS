Partner Standards

What We're Looking For

  • ✓

    Proven Experience

    Minimum 3 years in your trade with verifiable references and a portfolio of successfully completed projects.

  • ✓

    Licensing & Insurance

    Current trade licenses, general liability coverage, and workers compensation as required by Kenyan law.

  • ✓

    Quality Commitment

    Workmanship that meets or exceeds industry standards and all applicable building codes.

  • ✓

    Professional Equipment

    Well-maintained tools and equipment necessary to complete work to specification and on schedule.

  • ✓

    Communication Excellence

    Responsive to emails and calls with proactive updates on project status and any emerging issues.

  • ✓

    Safety First

    Strong safety record and unwavering commitment to OSHA standards and site-specific protocols.

  • ✓

    Team Collaboration

    Ability to coordinate effectively with other trades and respect shared project timelines.

  • ✓

    Problem Solving

    Proactive approach to identifying and resolving issues before they impact the delivery schedule.

Getting Started

Application Process

Submit Application

Complete the form below with your information, experience, and references. We review all submissions within 2 business days.

Day 1

Initial Interview

Phone or video call to discuss your experience, availability, and partnership goals. This is also your opportunity to learn about TRI-FORT.

Week 1

Verification & Site Visit

We verify licenses, insurance, and references. For certain trades, we may visit an active job site to observe your work firsthand.

Week 2

Onboarding & First Project

Complete paperwork, review our standards and procedures, then get assigned to your first project—typically a smaller job to ensure mutual fit.
