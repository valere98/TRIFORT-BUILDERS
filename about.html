Our Journey

Milestones That
Shaped Us

1987

Founded in Abu Dhabi

TRIFORT was established by the Khoury family with a vision to bring architectural integrity and craftsmanship to the Gulf region's rapidly growing skyline.

1998

First Landmark Commercial Project

Completion of the Al Manzil Tower marked our entry into large-scale commercial construction and established TRIFORT as a trusted regional contractor.

2007

International Expansion

We expanded operations into Dubai and Qatar, bringing our signature approach to luxury residential and hospitality projects across the broader GCC market.

2018

Sustainability Certification

TRIFORT became one of the first regional firms to achieve platinum-level green building certification across an entire residential portfolio.

Today

Building the Future

With over 420 completed projects and a portfolio spanning residential, commercial, and industrial sectors, we continue to set the benchmark for quality construction.

We do not merely construct buildings. We craft the environments in which lives unfold, businesses flourish, and legacies endure. — George Khoury, Founder
